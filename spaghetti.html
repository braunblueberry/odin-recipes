Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

This is a Spaghetti Aglio e Olio recipe. It's made with spaghetti, garlic, olive oil and chili flakes.

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Chili flakes
  • Parsley
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Add the chili flakes to the pan and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss to coat in the garlic and chili oil mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
  7. Serve immediately.
