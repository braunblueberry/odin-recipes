Burger

This is a cheeseburger recipe. It's made with brioche buns, cheddar cheese and pickles.

Ingredients:

  • Brioche buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Homemade Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Preheat the pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into patties and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Toast the brioche buns in the pan for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, pickles, cheese, and mayonnaise.
